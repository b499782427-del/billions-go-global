My Story

A few years ago, I was drowning in SaaS subscription costs. Between Shopify apps, hosting fees, and design tools, I was spending over $300/month before I even made my first sale. It was frustrating.

So I started digging. I tested alternatives, hunted for coupons, and figured out which tools were actually worth the money — and which ones were just good at marketing.

This blog is where I share everything I've learned.

What You'll Find Here

  • Shopify Deals — Honest comparisons of Shopify apps, themes, and tools. No sponsored fluff.
  • Hosting Coupons — Verified hosting discounts and real performance benchmarks.
  • Design Hacks — Practical tips to make your store look professional without hiring a designer.
