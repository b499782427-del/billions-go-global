You get access to basic voices and can create one custom voice. It's a great way to evaluate the quality before committing. **Best for:** Testing the platform, short projects, curious beginners. ## Starter Plan ($5/month) At just $5/month, the Starter plan is the most affordable way to use ElevenLabs commercially. You get 30,000 characters, HD voice quality, and the ability to create 10 custom voices. All audio generated can be used in commercial projects. **Best for:** Freelancers, small YouTube channels, podcast intros. ## Creator Plan ($22/month) The Creator plan is the sweet spot for most users. For $22/month, you get 100,000 characters and access to Pro voice quality — ElevenLabs' highest quality tier. You can create up to 30 custom voices and generate longer audio clips. **Best for:** Content creators, audiobook narrators, indie game developers. ## Pro Plan ($99/month) The Pro plan is designed for power users. At $99/month with 500,000 characters, you get everything ElevenLabs offers — Pro voices, unlimited custom voices, full API access, and priority generation. This is where you get the most value per character. **Best for:** Professional content studios, SaaS companies, publishing houses. ## Scale Plan ($330/month) The Scale plan is for growing businesses that need consistent, high-volume voice generation. With 2,000,000 characters and enterprise-grade support, it includes everything in Pro plus dedicated account management and faster processing. **Best for:** Growing businesses, localization agencies, large content teams. ## Enterprise Plan (Custom) For organizations with specific needs, the Enterprise plan offers custom pricing with dedicated infrastructure, on-premise deployment options, SSO authentication, custom SLAs, and personalized onboarding. **Best for:** Large enterprises, government agencies, organizations with strict data privacy requirements. ## Is ElevenLabs Worth It? For most content creators, the **Starter ($5) or Creator ($22) plan** offers excellent value. The voice quality significantly outperforms competitors like Murf AI and Play.ht at similar price points. The Free plan is generous enough to test thoroughly, and upgrading to paid unlocks commercial usage — making it easy to start small and scale up. If you're serious about AI voice content, ElevenLabs is currently the best value in the market. ## FAQs ### Does ElevenLabs offer a free trial? Yes. The Free plan includes 10,000 characters per month — no credit card required. You can test all the basic features before deciding to upgrade. ### Can I use ElevenLabs voices commercially? Yes. The main difference is voice quality. Starter gives you HD voices (good quality), while Creator unlocks Pro voices (ElevenLabs' highest quality).
