## Free Plan ($0)

The Free plan gives you 10,000 characters per month — enough to test the platform and create short voice clips. You get access to basic voices and can create one custom voice. It's a great way to evaluate the quality before committing.

**Best for:** Testing the platform, short projects, curious beginners.

## Starter Plan ($5/month)

At just $5/month, the Starter plan is the most affordable way to use ElevenLabs commercially. You get 30,000 characters, HD voice quality, and the ability to create 10 custom voices. All audio generated can be used in commercial projects.

**Best for:** Freelancers, small YouTube channels, podcast intros.

## Creator Plan ($22/month)

The Creator plan is the sweet spot for most users. For $22/month, you get 100,000 characters and access to Pro voice quality — ElevenLabs' highest quality tier. You can create up to 30 custom voices and generate longer audio clips.

**Best for:** Content creators, audiobook narrators, indie game developers.

## Pro Plan ($99/month)

The Pro plan is designed for power users. At $99/month with 500,000 characters, you get everything ElevenLabs offers — Pro voices, unlimited custom voices, full API access, and priority generation. This is where you get the most value per character.

## Scale Plan ($330/month)

The Scale plan is for growing businesses that need consistent, high-volume voice generation. With 2,000,000 characters and enterprise-grade support, it includes everything in Pro plus dedicated account management and faster processing.

## FAQs

### Does ElevenLabs offer a free trial?

Yes. The Free plan includes 10,000 characters per month — no credit card required. You can test all the basic features before deciding to upgrade.

### Can I use ElevenLabs voices commercially?

Yes. All paid plans (Starter and above) include full commercial usage rights. The Free plan is for personal/non-commercial use only.

### What's the difference between Starter and Creator?

The main difference is voice quality. Starter gives you HD voices (good quality), while Creator unlocks Pro voices (ElevenLabs' highest quality). Creator also triples your character limit from 30,000 to 100,000.

### Is there an annual discount?

ElevenLabs occasionally offers annual billing discounts. Check the pricing page for current offers — typically 20-30% off when billed annually.

### Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of the next billing cycle.
