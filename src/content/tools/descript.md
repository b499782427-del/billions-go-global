## What Is Descript?

Descript is an all-in-one AI-powered video and audio editor that lets you edit media by editing text. Instead of cutting and trimming on a timeline, you simply delete or rearrange words in the automatically generated transcript — and the video follows.

With 25+ languages, multi-speaker detection, and AI-powered tools like Studio Sound, Green Screen, and Eye Contact, Descript is the most innovative video editor on the market for content creators.
