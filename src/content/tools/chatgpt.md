## What Is ChatGPT

ChatGPT is OpenAI's flagship conversational AI model. Built on the GPT-4o architecture, it handles text generation, code writing, image understanding, data analysis, and creative tasks — all through a simple chat interface.

It's the most widely used AI tool globally, with over 300 million weekly active users as of 2026.

## Key Features

- **Multi-modal understanding** — Analyze images, PDFs, and documents alongside text
- **Code generation & debugging** — Write, explain, and fix code in all major programming languages
- **GPT-4o model** — Fast, accurate responses with 128K context window
- **Custom GPTs** — Build specialized AI assistants without coding
- **Web browsing** — Real-time information retrieval from the internet
- **DALL·E integration** — Generate images directly in chat
- **Voice conversations** — Natural speech interaction on mobile
- **Memory** — Remembers context across conversations

## Who Is It For

ChatGPT is for anyone who works with information. Content creators use it for writing and brainstorming. Developers use it for coding and debugging. Students use it for research and learning. Business professionals use it for analysis and communication.

It's particularly valuable for solo entrepreneurs and small teams who need an AI assistant but don't have the resources to build custom AI solutions.

## FAQ

### Is ChatGPT free?

Yes. ChatGPT offers a free tier with access to GPT-4o mini. You can upgrade to Plus ($20/month) for the full GPT-4o model, DALL·E image generation, and priority access during peak times.

### Is ChatGPT safe to use?

ChatGPT is generally safe for most use cases. OpenAI implements safety measures including content filters. For sensitive business data, use the Team or Enterprise plan which excludes your data from training.

### Can ChatGPT write code?

Yes. ChatGPT can write, explain, debug, and translate code across dozens of programming languages. It's widely used by developers for pair programming, though it may produce bugs in complex scenarios — always review AI-generated code.

### How does ChatGPT compare to Claude?

ChatGPT has broader features (image generation, voice, GPTs) and a stronger free tier. Claude excels at long-document analysis (200K context), nuanced reasoning, and safety. For most users, ChatGPT is more versatile; for enterprise and research use, Claude is often preferred.

### Does ChatGPT work offline?

No. ChatGPT requires an internet connection. The desktop and mobile apps also need connectivity — there's no offline mode.

### Can I use ChatGPT for commercial work?

Yes. Content created with ChatGPT can be used commercially. OpenAI's terms allow commercial use of outputs from both free and paid plans.
