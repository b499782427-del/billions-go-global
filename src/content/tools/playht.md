## What Is Play.ht?

Play.ht is an AI voice platform offering text-to-speech, voice cloning, and real-time voice conversion. With 900+ AI voices in 142 languages and accents, it has one of the largest voice libraries in the market.

It's particularly popular for audiobook creation, podcast production, and voice-enabled applications.
