## What Is Jasper AI?

Jasper is an AI writing assistant built specifically for marketing and business content. Unlike general-purpose AI like ChatGPT, Jasper is designed for copywriting — blog posts, social media, ad copy, emails, and landing pages.

With built-in brand voice profiles, SEO tools, and 50+ content templates, Jasper is the most focused AI writing tool for marketers.
