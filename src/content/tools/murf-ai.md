## What Is Murf AI?

Murf AI is a text-to-speech platform designed for professionals who need high-quality voiceovers. With 120+ AI voices across 20 languages, it's widely used for e-learning, corporate presentations, product demos, and YouTube videos.

Murf stands out for its studio-quality voices and user-friendly web-based editor that makes creating voiceovers as simple as typing text.
